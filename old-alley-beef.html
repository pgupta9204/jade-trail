Operating Hours: 24 Hours Daily 营业时间：24小时全天候

Fueled by a profound love for cooking, Hua Guomin has been serving his signature dishes to the local community for over 35 years. When their original location directly across the street faced demolition in 2009, they moved to this new spot without losing their loyal customer base. Staying true to his old-school roots, Hua Guomin refuses to use modern delivery apps, insisting that his food is best enjoyed fresh and hot right at the stall.
