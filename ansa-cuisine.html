Operating Hours: 10:00 - 03:00 Daily 营业时间：每天 10:00 - 03:00

Zhu Liping opened Ansa Cuisine just a year ago as a humble endeavor to make ends meet and support her family. Despite the shop's modest beginnings, her dedication to wok-firing delicious, filling meals has quickly drawn attention. Every bowl of stir-fry is cooked with immense care, proving that the best comfort food often comes from the most hardworking hands.

仅仅一年前，朱丽萍创办了安撒料理，初衷只是为了维持生计并补贴家用。尽管小店起步平凡，但她用心猛火翻炒出的美味饱腹之食，很快便吸引了食客们的目光。每一碗炒饭、炒面都倾注了她极大的心血，这也证明了最抚慰人心的美食往往出自最勤劳的双手。
